Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a free trial?

Yes! All plans come with a 14-day free trial. No credit card required to start.

How does Vendra compare to Shopify?

Vendra offers all the essential e-commerce features of Shopify but is optimized for Lebanese businesses with local payment Methods, Full support, and pricing in USD without international transaction fees.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept major Lebanese payment methods including OMT and Whish Money.

How can I activate my store?

Sign up to a new account, Login to the newly created account, fill up the form with the info to create your Store, then contact us to activate your subscription.
